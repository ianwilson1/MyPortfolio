Overview

SpotMe is a mobile application designed to help students, faculty, and staff at CSU Fresno locate available parking spots in real-time. The app enhances parking efficiency by providing live congestion data, soft reservation features, and a weekly scheduling tool to help users plan their parking habits. By integrating Google Maps API, MongoDB, and Google Cloud Services, SpotMe delivers a seamless, data-driven parking experience.
